About Me

This is what I do.

I am a student at Brigham Young University studying Pre-Marketing at the Marriott School of Business, where I maintain a 4.0 GPA and actively participate in the Finance Society, Marketing Association, and the Private Equity & Venture Capital Club. My academic background combines analytical rigor with creative problem-solving, preparing me to thrive in competitive business environments.

I have completed internships in private equity and venture capital, where I gained hands-on experience in financial analysis, market research, and investment evaluation. These experiences have strengthened my ability to assess opportunities and communicate insights with clarity and impact.

Beyond academics, I am passionate about leadership, service, and global engagement. I’ve lived abroad in Portugal, am conversationally fluent in Portuguese, and enjoy taking on challenges that help me grow personally and professionally. I’m driven, detail-oriented, and committed to making a meaningful impact wherever I go.
